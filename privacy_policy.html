Privacy Policy

📋 Introduction

Smart CRM ("we", "our", or "the app") is a professional contact and call management application designed for insurance field agents. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

By using Smart CRM, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Smart CRM is intended for use by authorised LIC / insurance employees only. All data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to a private Firebase cloud accessible only to your organisation's admin.

📊 Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Storage
Employee name, SR number, post, division, branch Profile setup and identification Local + Cloud (with consent)
Mobile phone number Profile verification via OTP Local + Cloud (with consent)
Contact names, phone numbers, policy details Call management and follow-ups Local + Cloud (with consent)
Call records and feedback Performance tracking and analytics Local + Cloud (with consent)
WhatsApp message logs Communication tracking Local + Cloud (with consent)
Follow-up schedules Reminder notifications Local only
Uploaded files (Excel, CSV, PDF, images) Contact import via OCR/parsing Processed locally, not stored

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality: contact management, call tracking, follow-ups
  • To verify your identity via Firebase Phone Authentication (OTP)
  • To sync your work data to your organisation's private cloud dashboard (only with your explicit consent)
  • To display analytics and performance reports to authorised admins
  • To send local notifications for scheduled follow-ups
  • To show a floating contact-reference widget during phone calls or WhatsApp messages

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside the app's core functionality.

🔐 App Permissions

Permission Why It Is Needed

☁️ Third-Party Services

Smart CRM uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – Used for phone number OTP verification. Firebase may process your phone number to send an SMS OTP. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Cloud Firestore – Used to store and sync employee profiles, contacts, call records, and feedback to your organisation's private database. Data is only synced when you explicitly enable "cloud sync" in the app. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google ML Kit (Text Recognition) – Used on-device to extract text from gallery images for contact import. No image data is sent to Google servers. ML Kit Terms

Firebase Analytics, Firebase Performance Monitoring, and Firebase Crashlytics are disabled in this app.

🔒 Data Storage & Security

  • All contact and call data is stored locally in an SQLite database on your device.
  • Cloud sync is opt-in only — you must explicitly enable it during profile setup.
  • Cloud data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with security rules that restrict access to your organisation's admin only.
  • All network communication uses HTTPS (TLS). Cleartext HTTP is disabled.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party outside your organisation.

👤 Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete all contacts and call records from within the app. To delete your cloud data, contact your IT administrator.
  • Opt-out of sync: You can disable cloud sync at any time by editing your profile and unchecking "Enable secure cloud sync".
  • Revoke overlay permission: You can revoke the "Display over other apps" permission at any time in Android Settings → Apps → Smart CRM → Permissions.

👶 Children's Privacy

Smart CRM is intended for use by adults (18+) who are employed as insurance agents. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.
